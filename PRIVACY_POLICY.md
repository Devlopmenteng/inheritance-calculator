## Introduction

Welcome to the Islamic Inheritance Calculator Pro (حاسبة المواريث الإسلامية - برو). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

**Important Note**: This app is designed with privacy in mind. All calculations are performed locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal inheritance data to external servers.

---

## Information We Collect

### 1. Information You Provide

- **Saved Calculations**: When you choose to save a calculation, it is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage.
- **User Preferences**: App settings (dark mode, selected madhab) are stored locally.

### 2. Automatically Collected Information

- **Audit Logs**: The app maintains an internal audit log of actions performed (e.g., "calculation performed", "test run") for your reference. These logs are stored locally and never transmitted.
- **Crash Reports**: If the app crashes, anonymous crash data may be collected through Firebase Crashlytics to help us improve stability.
- **Usage Analytics**: Anonymous usage statistics (e.g., feature usage, screen views) may be collected through Firebase Analytics to improve user experience.

### 3. What We DO NOT Collect

- ❌ Names of heirs or deceased persons
- ❌ Financial amounts entered (unless you explicitly save locally)
- ❌ Personal identification information
- ❌ Location data
- ❌ Contact information
- ❌ Device identifiers linked to personal data

---

## How We Use Your Information

### Local Data (On Your Device)

- **Saved Calculations**: To allow you to review previous inheritance calculations
- **Preferences**: To remember your app settings
- **Audit Logs**: To provide you with a history of app usage

### Anonymous Analytics (Firebase)

- **Crash Reports**: To identify and fix bugs
- **Usage Statistics**: To understand which features are most used and improve the app
- **Performance Data**: To optimize app performance

---

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage

- All personal data (saved calculations, preferences) is stored **only on your device**
- Data is stored using AsyncStorage (React Native's local storage)
- No encryption is applied to local data (consider this when using shared devices)

### Analytics Data

- Crashlytics and Analytics data is stored securely by Google Firebase
- Data is anonymized and aggregated
- Retention period: 90 days for analytics, 90 days for crash reports

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do **NOT**:

- Sell your data to third parties
- Share personal data with advertisers
- Transfer data outside your device (except anonymous analytics)

We may disclose information:

## Your Rights and Choices

### Access and Control

- **View Data**: All your data is visible within the app
- **Delete Data**: You can clear saved calculations and audit logs at any time from within the app
- **Export Data**: You can export audit logs from the app

### Opt-Out Options

- **Analytics**: You can disable analytics in app settings (if implemented)
- **Crash Reporting**: You can opt-out of crash reporting in device settings

### Data Deletion

To delete all your data:

1. Clear saved calculations in the app
2. Clear audit logs in the app
3. Uninstall the app to remove all local data
